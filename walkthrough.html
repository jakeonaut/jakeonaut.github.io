Step by step, spoiler free walkthrough!!!

This walkthrough is broken into five parts. The first three sections can be completed in any order, whereas the fourth and fifth sections need to be completed in order, after the first three have been done.

At any time during rooms I-IV, type "left" or "right" and press enter to cycle between rooms.
----------------------------------------------------------------------

I. THE BEARD ROOM

1. > "read tome"
2. copy the final line of the tomb text, right before the three exclamation marks.
    -it should look something like KGDH JH RAH OHC
3. > "look lion eye"
4. > "yes"
5. paste and enter the copied text from the previous step.
6. you now have the BEARD KEY!
7. use "left" or "right" to enter another incomplete room.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

II. THE CAT ROOM

1. > "console woman"
2. Keep entering "y" while she says "Meow meow meow meow" (exactly 4 meows).
3. When she says "Meow meow meow meow MEOOOOOOW!!!!", enter "n"
4. you now have the CAT KEY!
5. use "left" or "right" to enter another incomplete room.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

III. THE BED ROOM

1. > "jump bed"
2. > "jump bed"
3. just kidding... sorry!

1. > "sleep bed"
2. you can type anything here..
3. > "kill"
4. you now have the DREAM KEY!
5. use "left" or "right" to enter another incomplete room.
